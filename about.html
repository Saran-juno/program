Tomato Rub Face Pack

Tomato face pack work as a natural astringent that helps tighten the skin as well as shrink large pores, leaving the skin tight and glowing.This mask helps remove dead skin cells, dirt, and excess oil from your skin, leaving it soft and glowing. Tomato Cucumber face pack

This face mask is very effective in geting rid of tanned skin. Mix fresh juice of tomato and cucumber in equal quantities and apply this mixture on the affected area. Allow it to air dry for 10 minutes and wash it off with clean water.

Tomato with sugar mask

Curd with Honey Face pack

Works as a natural exfoliator and natural moisturiser it Provides face and skin hydration.Mix 2 tablespoons of curd with a tablespoon of honey. Apply this mixture to your face and leave it on for about 20 minutes. Wash the mixture off with cold water later.

Rice flour with castor oil

Rice flour packs are extremely beneficial in maintaining oil levels in the skin. Rice flour with Aloa vera
